We're

Divine.

Divine Chocolatier is Master Chocolatier and wife Chef owned business. We (They) created this business out of love for the finest chocolate possible. If we are going to indulge in dessert we make sure the calories count in the form of extreme decadence pleasure.

Divine Chocolatier is a private company that is not franchised. We make small productions of all our products to ensure constant freshness. All our products are hand produced using only the finest cocoa beans from different regions and are house fused for the best end flavour and aroma. “Divine Chocolatier is an unpretentious boutique that locals love and tourists seek out” (by the Candyatlas). We are proud to serve and ship internationally.

Chocolate should be judged by the quality of the cocoa beans and not the percentage of the cocoa butter. But in general for a perfect balance of intense flavour of chocolate and the pleasure of the finest dessert we recommend 70 to 72%.

Divine Chocolatier has had the privilege of serving many known celebrities, to name a few; Jennifer Love Hewitt, Penelope Cruz and husband Javier Bardem, Johnny Depp, William Shatner, Robert De Niro, Pierre Trudeau.
