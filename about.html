About Divine Chocolatier

Divine chocolatier offers artisanal handmade chocolates, with a selection of gift boxes or indiivdual chocolates. Potential customers would be chocolate lovers from people looking for a gift for a friend or looking for a treat for themselves. They also specialize in creating gift boxes for weddings or parties, so party planners are also a potential customer. The appeal of how the product is showcase is how it would entice people to purchase the product. This would mean showing high close up details of the chocolates, framing them so they look delicious enough that that the customer wants them.
